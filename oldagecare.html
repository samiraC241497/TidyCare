Our Services

Personal hygiene and grooming assistance

Service Overview: Our Personal Hygiene and Grooming Assistance service provides gentle, respectful care to help seniors maintain their well-being and dignity. Our trained caregivers assist with daily hygiene routines, ensuring comfort, cleanliness, and confidence.

Key Features:

  • Bathing and Dressing Assistance
  • Oral and Skincare Support
  • Hair and Nail Care
  • Mobility and Toileting Aid

Price: Price: 3,038 BDT/hour

Medication reminders and health monitoring

Service Overview: Our Medication Reminders and Health Monitoring service ensures seniors stay on top of their health with caring, reliable support. Our caregivers provide gentle reminders for medications and keep an eye on vital health indicators, promoting overall wellness and peace of mind

Key Features:

  • Timely Medication Reminders
  • Monitoring of Vitals (e.g., blood pressure, glucose)
  • Health Log and Progress Tracking
  • Communication with Family or Healthcare Providers

Price: Price:3,645 BDT/hour

Meal preparation and nutritional guidance

Service Overview:Our Meal Preparation and Nutritional Guidance service offers wholesome, home-cooked meals tailored to individual dietary needs. Our caregivers prepare balanced dishes while providing expert advice to support seniors' health and well-being.

Key Features:

  • Personalized Meal Planning
  • Nutrient-Rich Recipe Selection
  • Special Diet Accommodation (e.g., low-sodium, diabetic-friendly)
  • Portion Control and Hydration Support

Price: Price: 3,038 BDT/meal

Companionship and emotional support

Service Overview:Our Companionship and Emotional Support service provides warm, heartfelt connection to brighten each day. Our caregivers offer meaningful conversations, engaging activities, and a comforting presence to reduce loneliness and promote emotional well-being.

Key Features:

  • Friendly Conversations and Active Listening
  • Recreational Activities and Hobbies
  • Emotional Encouragement and Mental Stimulation
  • Accompaniment for Walks and Appointments

Price: Price: 2,430 BDT/hour

Mobility assistance and light physical exercises

Service Overview: Our Mobility Assistance and Light Physical Exercises service helps seniors stay active and independent. Our caregivers provide gentle support for safe movement and guide simple exercises to enhance strength, balance, and flexibility.

Key Features:

  • Walking and Transfer Assistance
  • Stretching and Low-Impact Exercises
  • Fall Prevention and Safety Monitoring
  • Personalized Activity Plans

Price: Price: 3,645 BDT/hour
